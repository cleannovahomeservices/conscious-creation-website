The CurriculumWhat the Course Will Cover

Module One

The Three Foundational Recognitions

The ground beneath all conscious creation. Wholeness as your nature. Consciousness as causal. The Self as the doer. Three lessons, one for each recognition, with contemplations and embodied practices.

Module Two

The Seven Paired Teachings

The full architecture: identity, vision, assumption, feeling, embodiment, surrender, love. Each teaching paired with its movement. Seven lessons, walking principle into practice.

Module Three

Recognising the Diversions

The seven common patterns that block conscious creation. How each one feels from the inside, why each one fails to manifest, and the remedy that returns the practitioner to the I AM.

Module Four

The Final Recognition & Living Beyond Practice

What it means when the practice has become the practitioner. How to live beyond formal practice. How to continue when the daily ritual has dissolved into the way you naturally are.

For WhomWho the Course Is For

The course is for those who have read the books and want to walk deeper — to hear the teaching in the teacher's voice, to see the embodied practices demonstrated, to receive contemplations and reflections that the page cannot fully carry.

It is not for beginners to spirituality. It is not a "manifestation course" in the contemporary sense. It is a teaching course for serious practitioners who recognize that the work is at the level of identity, and who want a sustained immersion in the principles.
