How to Use ThemWalking the Two Together

The two booklets are designed to be walked together. The teaching booklet is the map; the practice booklet is the steady walking. Most readers begin with the teaching booklet — reading it once for the architecture, then again, more slowly, for the depth. When the teaching has settled, the practice booklet opens for the twenty-one days of embodiment.

Some readers go in the other order — beginning with the practice and letting the teaching unfold through doing it. Both ways work. The teaching and the practice point at the same Self.

A Note

These are not motivational books. They are teaching documents. They will work to the degree you sit with them slowly — and not at all if you read them once and put them away.
