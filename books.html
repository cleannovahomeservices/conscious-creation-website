How the Books Work Together

The library is sequenced. Each book opens what the next builds upon. You may begin anywhere — but if you ask where to start, here is the path.

  1. i.
    The Way of Conscious Creation — for the recognition. The teaching itself. Read first. Free.
  2. ii.
    The Practice — Twenty-One Days — for the embodiment. Walks the principles into the body and the day. Companion to the first.
  3. iii.
    Universal Principles of Life — for the framework. Eighty-four operating laws across ten domains of life. The wider field of principle.
  4. iv.
    The Ascension Manual — for the deeper work. The path of remembering, the I AM Presence, the Violet Flame, transmission and embodiment.
  5. v.
    The Universal One Synthesis — for the cosmological floor. The metaphysics of creation in the language of cosmic science.

The teaching is freely given. What carries a price is the labor — the years of practice, the months of writing, the form into which the recognition has been placed for you. This honors both the recognition and the labor.
