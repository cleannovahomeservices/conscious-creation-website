The App

The Practice AppYour conscious creation companion

A gentle daily companion that holds the rhythm of conscious creation — morning, day, evening — quietly, in your pocket.

Where the books give you the teaching and the twenty-one day practice, the app extends the practice into the rhythm of every day. Not a tracker. Not a coach. A companion.

The Daily RhythmThree Returns

The app moves with the natural rhythm of a day. Three gentle returns — morning, day, evening — each holding you in the practice without ever pushing.

What Lives InsideThe Companion's Features

Morning Anchor

Intention Setting

Before the world rises, the app holds a quiet space to set the day's I AM and carry one contemplation through every hour that follows.

Daily Prompts

Gentle Returns

Throughout the day, brief prompts arrive — a breath, a phrase, a recognition. Never demanding. Always optional. The companion never insists.

Evening Release

The Day's Close

Before sleep, a short ritual to bless what was lived, release the form, and sleep into the assumption. Three minutes. The body learns through the night.

Letters from Constantin

Occasional Teachings

Short audio and written letters arrive when meaningful — never on a content schedule. A teaching, a contemplation, a word for the moment.

The Library

Teachings on Demand

The full architecture from The Way of Conscious Creation, indexed for return. When a diversion appears, the remedy is a tap away.

Recognitions

A Gentle Practice Path

As you walk the practice, the app marks recognitions — moments of return, of presence, of conscious creation. Not a game. A quiet record of the path.

On the Recognitions

The app loves the practitioner. The app does not manipulate them. There are no streaks to lose. No notifications designed to addict. The Recognitions are simply a quiet acknowledgement that you are walking the path — earned through showing up, never through performance.

The Companion's PromiseHow It Holds You

A Companion, Not a Coach

The voice never pushes.

The app accompanies. It does not motivate, does not remind you what you "should" do, does not perform spirituality. When you open it, you are met with stillness — the same register the books carry, distilled to fit a screen.

Designed for the Practitioner

Not a self-improvement product.

The app is built for those who already understand that the work is at the level of identity, not behaviour. It does not gamify your soul. It supports a daily rhythm of return to the I AM that you already are.

The Books Are the On-Ramp

Read first. Walk after.

The app is the natural continuation of The Practice — Twenty-One Days of Embodiment. Walk those twenty-one days first in the booklet. Then continue, indefinitely, in the app. Same rhythm, now portable.
