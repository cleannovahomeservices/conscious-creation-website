For those who want more than the page can give. The same teaching, in the container that meets your life — begin where you are.

These ways of working are not arranged in a hierarchy. Each is whole on its own. Begin where you are, stay as long as it serves, and move when life calls you forward.

The Focused Container

The Group

A time-bound walking of the full architecture, together

A focused container in which a small cohort walks the whole Conscious Creation architecture together, one teaching at a time, over several weeks. Structured and concentrated, with Constantin guiding the group directly — embodied practices between sessions, and a circle of others holding the work alongside you.

By the close, you have not only read the teaching. You have lived it, in company.

The Direct Contact

One to One

Individual work, by appointment

For moments when life is asking for personal contact rather than group support. Individual sessions, online, working directly at the level of identity. This is conscious work with the consciousness from which your life is presently arising, rather than advice-giving or problem-solving in the ordinary sense.

Single sessions or a short series, by appointment. Availability is limited; if it is not currently open, you are welcome to begin in the community while you wait.

How to ChooseWhich one meets you

There is no wrong door, and none is the on-ramp to another. If you are new to the work, begin in the community — the commitment is light and you may stay as long as it serves. If you want focused, structured work, walk the group through the full architecture with a cohort. If life is asking for personal contact, work one to one.

A Word

Each of these is a complete way of working. None requires you to "graduate" toward anything else. Choose what serves your life now, and trust that life will tell you when something else is asked.
