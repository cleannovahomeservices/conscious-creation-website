Coaching

Direct SupportFour ways to walk together

The same teaching, in four containers. Community, group program, ongoing circle, individual sessions. Choose the form that meets you.

The coaching offerings are not a ladder you climb toward something more important. Each is whole on its own. Begin where you are. Stay as long as it serves. Move when life calls you forward.

One · The Entry

The Community

Ongoing access to the living teaching

The Community is the entry door — accessible, ongoing, and held lightly. A monthly membership that gives you a steady connection to the teaching, the practice, and the larger field of practitioners walking the same way.

What It Holds

Monthly group calls with Constantin — teachings, contemplations, and a space to bring questions from your daily practice. A library of recorded sessions. Written meditations and decrees delivered to your inbox. A small private community of practitioners walking the same path.

This is the most accessible way to begin working with Constantin directly. Many practitioners stay in the Community indefinitely; others use it as a foundation before stepping into a program or circle.

Two · The Focused Container

The Group Program

A time-bound walking of the full architecture

The Group Program is a focused, time-bound container in which a small cohort walks the full Conscious Creation architecture together over six to twelve weeks. Structured. Concentrated. With Constantin guiding the cohort directly each week.

What It Holds

Weekly group calls with Constantin walking through one teaching at a time. Embodied practices and contemplations between sessions. A small cohort holding each other in the practice. By the end, you have lived the entire architecture in community.

Three · The Intimate Continuation

The Circle

An ongoing intimate group, by invitation

The Circle is for those who have walked the Group Program or worked individually with Constantin and want to continue in an ongoing intimate container. A small, ongoing group — six to ten people — that meets regularly to deepen the work over months and years rather than weeks.

What It Holds

Twice-monthly intimate calls with Constantin and the small group. Personal application of the teaching to whatever is alive in each practitioner's life. A held container in which mastery ripens — not rushed, not pushed, simply attended to over time.

Four · The Direct Contact

Individual Sessions

One-to-one work, by appointment

For those who want direct individual work — moments where life is calling for personal contact rather than group support. Single sessions or packages, by appointment, addressing what is alive for you specifically.

What It Holds

Sixty-minute individual sessions, online. Direct work at the level of identity. The session is principle-rooted and elevated — not advice-giving, not problem-solving in the conventional sense, but conscious work with the consciousness from which your life is currently arising.

How to ChooseWhich Container Meets You

There is no wrong door. Each offering is whole on its own. But here is rough guidance, if you want it.

If you are new to the work

Begin in the Community.

The monthly commitment is light, the entry is gentle, and you can stay as long as it serves. Many practitioners spend years in the Community before — or instead of — moving to anything more intensive.

If you want focused, structured work

Apply for the Group Program.

Six to twelve weeks of concentrated walking through the full architecture, with a cohort. Best if you have read the books and want to embody the practice with Constantin's direct guidance.

If you want ongoing intimate work in community

The Circle is the home.

For those who have walked the Program or worked individually and want to continue. Slow, steady, deep. The container in which mastery ripens over months and years.

If you want direct one-to-one contact

Book a session.

For specific moments of life that call for personal work, or as a complement to any of the other containers. Single sessions or packages.

A Word

None of these is the on-ramp to anything else. None requires you to "graduate" toward the Sovereign Year. Each is a complete way of working. Choose what serves your life now, and trust that life will tell you when something else is asked.
