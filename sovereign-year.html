An Invitation

The Sovereign YearFor the twelve who are ready

A yearlong personal container with Constantin Cranz, directly. Not a program. Not a course. A walking together — for those for whom this work has become what life is asking.

This is the deepest work offered. It does not live here.

The Sovereign Year is not part of the Conscious Creation teaching brand. It is a container of direct personal mentorship with Constantin Cranz — his time, his attention, his discernment, his year. Because it is fundamentally a relationship with the teacher rather than the teaching itself, it lives on his personal site, where it belongs.

This page is an invitation, and a door. If something in you stirs while reading this, follow the door at the bottom of the page. You will arrive at constantincranz.com — a single page, a single offering, a single application. From there, what unfolds will unfold.

What This Is

A year. Twelve practitioners. Direct work with Constantin. By application. Held privately.

A Word on the YearWhat This Container Holds

The Sovereign Year is a private, year-long container in which a small cohort of twelve practitioners walks directly with Constantin Cranz toward the embodied recognition of their own sovereign creation. It is not a curriculum that ends. It is a year that is walked. The work moves through cycles, deepens through seasons, lands somewhere different than where it began.

Time itself is part of the container. Six months in, what is happening cannot be made to happen in six weeks. A year, with proper containing, makes possible what shorter containers cannot.

One

Direct Personal Contact

Significant individual time with Constantin throughout the year, in addition to the small-group field. Not a coaching program in the contemporary sense. A relationship, held with rigor.

Two

Twelve, Carefully Chosen

The cohort is held at twelve — small enough that each is fully attended to, large enough that the field carries. Not chosen by who applies first. Chosen by who is ready.

Three

An Investment of Substance

Significant — financial, energetic, temporal. This is intentional. Practitioners who arrive at this work have generally moved through what shorter, lower-investment work can deliver, and are ready for something whose weight matches the depth of what is being asked.

Who This Is ForAnd Who It Is Not

This work is for those who have walked some form of inner work for years, perhaps decades. Those who have read the books, walked the practices, and recognise that what is being offered here is what life is now asking of them. Those who can hold the rigor of a year — financially, temporally, energetically. Those who do not need to be persuaded.

This is not for those new to inner work. It is not for those looking for transformation in a single offering. It is not for those who want a lower-cost version of the same teaching. The teaching itself is freely available — through the books, the app, the writings — at no cost. The other coaching containers offer the teaching at every accessible level, and many practitioners find their entire practice fully held there. The Sovereign Year is one offering among several. Not the destination. Simply a particular form for a particular moment.
