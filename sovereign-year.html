The Sovereign Year

For the TwelveWho Are Ready

A one-year container for those prepared to step fully into sovereign creation. Twelve participants. By application. Details unfolding.

This is the deepest work offered.

The Sovereign Year is a private, year-long container in which twelve practitioners walk directly with Constantin Cranz toward the embodied recognition of their own sovereign creation. It is not a course. It is not a coaching program in the contemporary sense. It is a year — fully held, fully inhabited — in which the work moves from understanding to identity, from practice to nature, from doing to being.

The form of the year is held privately. Participants enter not knowing exactly what each month will hold; only that the year will deliver them somewhere they could not arrive on their own.

An Invitation to Inquire

The full form of this work is shared in conversation, not published. To inquire is to begin. If you are reading this and feel called, write.

The ContainerWhat This Is

One

A Year, Not a Program

This is not a curriculum that ends. It is a year that is walked. The work moves through cycles, deepens through seasons, and lands in a different place than where it began. Time itself is part of the container.

Two

Twelve Practitioners

The cohort is held at twelve — small enough that each is fully attended to, large enough that the field carries. The twelve are chosen carefully. Not by who applies first, but by who is ready.

Three

Direct Contact with Constantin

Significant individual contact throughout the year, alongside the held group field. The work is held at the level of identity, not circumstance. The intention is not problem-solving but the awakening of the one in whom all good already lives.

Four

An Investment of Substance

The year is a significant investment — financial, energetic, temporal. This is intentional. Participants who arrive at this work have generally moved through what shorter-term, lower-investment work can deliver, and are ready for something whose weight matches the depth of what is being asked.

For WhomWho This Is For

Those who have walked some form of inner work for years, perhaps decades. Those who have read the books, walked the practices, and recognise that what is being offered here is what life is now asking of them. Those who can hold the rigor of a year — financially, temporally, energetically. Those who do not need to be persuaded.

If you are reading this page and recognising yourself, the year may be for you. If you are reading this page and feeling pressured or sold to, the year is not for you — and nothing else on this site is, either, in that register.

For Whom This Is NotA Word of Honesty

This is not for those new to inner work. It is not for those looking for transformation in a single offering. It is not for those who want a lower-cost version of the same. The other containers in Coaching offer the teaching at every accessible level, and many practitioners find their entire practice fully held there. There is no shame, no hierarchy, no implied "next level." The Sovereign Year is one offering among several. Not the destination — simply a particular form for a particular moment.

If You Are CalledHow to Inquire

Inquiry is by personal letter — written, sent to the email below. Tell Constantin who you are, what work you have walked to this point, and why this year, in this moment, is what you are being asked to step into.

Application is not a sales process. It is a conversation. You will hear back, you will have a call, and from that call you and Constantin will both know whether the year is yours.
