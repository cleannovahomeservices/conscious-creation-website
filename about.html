About

Constantin CranzThe teacher behind the teaching

Working at the level of identity and consciousness — in service of those ready to remember.

For more than twenty-five years, Constantin Cranz has practiced and taught one recognition, returned to again and again: that nothing is missing, that the I AM you are is already whole, and that a life is created from that fullness — never forged from lack.

The work is a synthesis. It draws the same Light from the contemplative traditions of East and West — the non-dual recognition, the way of the awakened heart, the law of assumption, the cosmology of the One — and speaks it in one contemporary voice, for the one who is ready to hear it. The teaching is universal. The synthesis is ours.

The ApproachHow the work is held

The teaching does not motivate. It does not push, persuade, or perform. It names what is true — that the I AM is whole, that the inner shapes the outer, that imagination is the creative faculty of the Self — and then it steps aside. Once these are recognized, the work becomes the quiet art of allowing what is already true at the level of being to express itself in the daily life.

Less the management of circumstance than the remembering of the one in whom all good already lives. Sessions and writings are practical, principle-rooted, and dignified in tone — never advice piled on the personality, but a return to the Self that was never lost.

The Ground It Rests On

Three recognitions

Identity precedes creation. You do not create what you want; you create what you are. The work begins not with action but with the identity from which all action arises.

The inner shapes the outer. Consciousness is causal; the world is reflection. This is not a power the ego wields — it is a depth the ego surrenders to. To shift the form, attend first to the consciousness from which the form arises.

You are not the doer. The personal will does not create — it aligns with the creative power already moving through it. Strain is the signature of false agency. Ease is the signature of alignment.

Where It Comes FromA weaving, not an invention

The principles here have been spoken in many tongues across many ages — by saints, sages, and seers, in traditions that each carry their own depth and their own history. The work names that synthesis without dissolving into any single source: it gathers what the lineages point toward and articulates it, plainly, for the present.

It asks no allegiance. It does not ask you to convert, and it does not ask you to believe. It asks only that you test, in your own direct experience, what the traditions have always pointed at — that you are already what you have been seeking. The names at the back of the books honor the influences; they are offered as invitations, not authorities.

A Word

The brand is not the personality of the teacher. The brand is the teaching. The work is to point to the I AM you already are — and then to step aside.

The WorkWho Constantin works with

He works with those ready to step out of seeking and into conscious creation — people who sense that their lives are an expression of consciousness and wish to live deliberately, in alignment with life. Often they have already done real inner work, and are ready for a word that can carry weight without weighing them down.

Constantin lives and works from Medellín, Colombia, and works internationally, across many cultures and contexts. The longer, year-long path — the Sovereign Year — lives on its own site at constantincranz.com.
