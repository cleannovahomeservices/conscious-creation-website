About

Constantin CranzThe teacher behind the teaching

Working at the level of identity and consciousness, in service of those ready to remember.

For more than thirty years, Constantin Cranz has practiced and taught the principles of Ascension — drawing on a synthesis of teachings from the I AM Discourses, A Course in Miracles, the Way of Mastery, Neville Goddard's Law of Assumption, Walter Russell's cosmology of the One, Advaita Vedanta, Tibetan Bon and Dzogchen, Hridaya Yoga, and the broader contemplative lineages.

His coaching is grounded in universal principles, not techniques. It rests on three recognitions: that identity precedes behaviour, that the inner creates the outer, and that imagination is the primary creative tool of the Self. The work is causal — not the management of circumstance, but the awakening of the one in whom all good already lives.

The ApproachHow the Work Is Held

The teaching does not motivate. It does not push, persuade, or perform. It simply names what is true — that the I AM is whole, that consciousness is causal, that the Self extends Itself in form. Once these are recognized, the work becomes the conscious art of allowing what is already true at the level of being to express itself in the daily life.

Sessions are practical, principle-rooted, and elevated in tone. The aim is not to add to the personality but to remember the Self that was never lost.

The Three Recognitions

That ground all the work

Identity precedes behaviour. What you do follows from what you have already taken yourself to be. Therefore the work begins not with action, but with the assumption of the identity you wish to live from.

The inner creates the outer. Consciousness is causal; the world is reflection. To shift the form, shift the consciousness. There is no other way.

Imagination is the primary creative tool. Imagination is not fantasy — it is the creative function of the One Mind, locally expressing through you. To imagine, with feeling, is to participate in the creative act.

The LineagesWhere the Teaching Comes From

The work is a synthesis, not an invention. The principles articulated here have been spoken in many tongues across many traditions. What is offered is a weaving — drawing from the lineages above to articulate, in contemporary language, what the saints, sages, and seers have always taught.

The teaching does not require allegiance to any single tradition. It does not ask you to convert. It does not ask you to believe. It asks only that you recognize, in your own direct experience, what the lineages have always pointed toward: that you are already what you have been seeking.

The WorkWho Constantin Works With

He works with those who are ready to step out of seeking and into sovereign creation — individuals who sense that their lives are the expression of consciousness, and who wish to live deliberately, in alignment with God, life, and the universe. Not beginners. Not those looking to be sold to.

His clients tend to be people who have already done significant inner work and are now ready for the teacher whose word can carry weight without weighing them down. The sessions are direct, principle-rooted, and held at the level of identity rather than circumstance.

Constantin is based in Medellín, Colombia, and works internationally with clients across many cultures and contexts.

A Word

The brand is not the personality of the teacher. The brand is the teaching. Constantin's work is to point to the I AM that you already are — and then to step aside.
