Forthcoming · Essay

Why "Manifestation" Was Never the Word

The word has been worn thin by a market that promises outcomes it cannot give. A reflection on the truer one beneath it — creation — and why nothing, in the end, is being acquired.

Forthcoming · Teaching

Wanting, and the Turn into Receiving

On the most common diversion of all — wanting from lack — and the quiet inner turn that lets wanting dissolve in the having. With contemplations to sit with.

Forthcoming · Reflection

Making and Creating

One distinction carries the whole teaching: the ego makes, the Self creates. A close look at how the difference is felt in the body, long before either word is spoken.

Forthcoming · Essay

On Living from the I AM

An extended contemplation on what it means, in daily life, to live from the I AM rather than the personality — not as a technique, but as the natural posture of the one who has remembered.

A Letter to Those in the Middle of the Practice

For those past the first recognition and not yet through to the deeper one. A word for the in-between — where the slipping does not undo the work, and the returning is the work.
